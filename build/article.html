How Do I use the YouTube Content App?

This content app allows you to display YouTube videos on your digital signage screen.

What You'll Need

A compatible digital signage player e.g. Xhibit, BrightSign, etc.XhibitSignage Software with a supported feature packageInternet Connectivity Access to YouTube

Using YouTube

Hover over the "MEDIA" menu item and click on "ADD MEDIA." Click on the "WEB" category and select the "YouTube" app. In the YouTube app, there is a selection of popular channels, as well as different categories of channels such as News, Sports, or Education. Once you have selected a channel, click on the "PREVIEW" button to see a preview in the right hand box. To add a custom YouTube video, playlist, or channel, click on the "heart" category. For more information on how to paste your video, hover over the "YouTube Video ID" label. For options to mute audio, click on the "ADVANCED SETTINGS" button circled in red. You may also use the Closed Captions toggle to pull any Closed Captions/Subtitles from the video. *NOTE* Any video you would like to use subtitles on will need to have the Closed Captions created and set by the video creator. Please see here for additional information to create these Closed Captions.Once you are satisfied with your content, you may name it within the "Name / Title" box Then click on the green "SAVE" button to add your YouTube video to the media library
